Technological Failures

Technological Failures :

These disasters result from the failure or malfunction of technological systems and equipment. Examples include airplane crashes, train derailments, and infrastructure failures such as bridge collapses.

Causes :

1.Industrial pollution
2. Nuclear radiation
3.Toxic wastes
4.Dam failures
5.Transportation accidents
6.Factory explosion
7.Fires
8.Chemical spills.

Prevention :

1.Ensure Employees Are Aware Of The Signs Of Failure.
2.Install Authentic Software.
3.Check If All Cords Are Plugged In Properly.
4.Use Quality Parts to Prevent IT Equipment Failure.
5.Keep Hardware And System Software Up To Date.
6.Implement A Preventative Maintenance Schedule.
