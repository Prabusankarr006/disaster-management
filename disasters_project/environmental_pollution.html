Environmental Pollution

Environmental Pollution :

Pollution of air, water, and soil through human activities can have far-reaching consequences. Examples of man-made environmental disasters include oil spills, air pollution from factories and vehicles, and contamination of drinking water sources.
These are further classified into the following types of pollution:

  1. Soil pollution
  2. Air pollution
  3. Land pollution
  4. Noise pollution

Causes :

1.Deforestation
2.Burning of bushes dumping of agricultural
3. Household wastes in water bodies
4. Use of chemicals in harvesting aquatic animals
5.Improper disposal of electronic wastes

Preventive measures:

1. Reduce the number of trips you take in your car.
2.Reduce or eliminate fireplace and wood stove use.
3.Avoid burning leaves, trash, and other materials
3.Avoid using gas-powered lawn and garden equipment.
