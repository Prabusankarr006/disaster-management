Humanitarian Disaster

Humanitarian Disaster :

A humanitarian emergency or disaster refers to a situation in which there is a sudden and significant disruption to the normal functioning of a community or society, leading to a widespread crisis that requires urgent humanitarian assistance. These emergencies can result from various natural or man-made events and typically involve the potential or actual loss of life, displacement of people, and damage to infrastructure and essential services. Humanitarian emergencies can have a severe impact on the affected population's well-being, health, and livelihoods.

Types :

  1. Flood
  2. Epidemics
  3. Tsunami
  4. Drought
  5. Wildfire
