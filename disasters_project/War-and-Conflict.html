War and Conflict

War and Conflict :

Armed conflicts, including wars and civil wars, can lead to widespread destruction and displacement of populations. The deliberate targeting of civilian populations and infrastructure in conflict zones contributes to man-made disasters

Causes :

These fundamental or primary causes of war explain why war repeatedly occurs in international politics, why war can occur at any moment.
Thus scholars trace war to human nature, biological instincts, frustration, fear and greed, the existence of weapons, and similar factors.

Prevention :

It is thus possible that international organizations can contribute to the prevention of wars by devising and institutionalizing alternative, peaceful techniques for the settlement of disputes and by persuading the states to use them.
