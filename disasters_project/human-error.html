Human Error

Human Error :

Accidents resulting from human mistakes, misjudgments, or negligence can lead to disasters. This category includes incidents like plane crashes due to pilot error, medical errors, and accidents in hazardous occupations.

Causes :

Poor training, poor management, poorly written operating or maintenance procedures, and improper work tools.
There are three general types - skill-based, rule-based, and knowledge-based errors.

Prevention :

1.Identify error-prone parts of your business.
2.Employ automation where you can.
3.Commit to continuous employee training.
4.Create detailed process documents.
5.Invest in an offsite cloud data backup.
6.Improve internal communication lines.
7.Use the Principle of Least Privilege.
