Disaster Management

Disaster management, also known as emergency management or crisis management, is a systematic and coordinated process of preparing for, mitigating the impact of, responding to, and recovering from disasters and emergencies. Its primary goal is to protect lives, 'property, and the environment during and after various types of disasters and emergencies, whether they are natural or man-made. Disaster management involves a range of activities and measures to minimize the negative effects of such events and facilitate a swift and effective response.
