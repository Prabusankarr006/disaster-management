Environmental Disaster

Definition:

An environmental disaster refers to a catastrophic event or situation that causes significant harm to the environment, ecosystems, and often human populations. These disasters can have wide-ranging and long-lasting effects on the natural world, leading to ecological imbalances, loss of biodiversity, and damage to ecosystems.
